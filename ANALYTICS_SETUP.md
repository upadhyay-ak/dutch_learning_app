# Setting Up Google Analytics

## Step 1: Create Google Analytics Account

1. Go to https://analytics.google.com/
2. Click "Start measuring"
3. Create an account:
   - Account name: "Dutch Flashcards"
   - Check "Web" platform
4. Property details:
   - Property name: "Dutch Learning App"
   - Reporting timezone: Your timezone
   - Currency: EUR
5. Business information: (select appropriate options)
6. Click "Create"

## Step 2: Get Your Measurement ID

After creating, you'll see a **Measurement ID** like: `G-XXXXXXXXXX`

Copy this ID!

## Step 3: Add to Your App

I'll automatically add the tracking code to your index.html with placeholder.
Replace `G-XXXXXXXXXX` with your actual ID.

## What You'll Track (Free Forever):

✅ **Users:** Daily/monthly active users
✅ **Page views:** How many times flashcards are viewed
✅ **Geography:** Where users are from
✅ **Devices:** Mobile vs desktop usage
✅ **Engagement:** Time spent on app
✅ **Popular cards:** Which levels (A1/A2) are most used

## Privacy Note:

Add to your footer: "This site uses Google Analytics for usage statistics."
